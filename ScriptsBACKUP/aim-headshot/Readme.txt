How to add this map:

1)  Open aim-headshot.pwn with pawn or text editor, copy everything.

2) Open [name of your gamemode you use].pwn and paste under public OnGameModeInit()

3) Do same for Weapon Pickups and Spawns.

4) Compile.

5) Have Fun!
